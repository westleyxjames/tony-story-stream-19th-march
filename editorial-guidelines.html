Editorial Guidelines

At StoryStream Daily, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of editorial integrity and transparency. Our editorial guidelines ensure that all content meets our quality standards and serves our readers' best interests.

Fact-Checked Information

We verify facts and cite credible sources where applicable to ensure accuracy in our reporting.

No Exaggerated Claims

We avoid sensationalism and present information in a balanced, truthful manner.

No Medical or Financial Promises

Our content is informational only and does not provide medical advice, financial guarantees, or professional recommendations.

Clear Distinction Between Content Types

We clearly label sponsored content, advertisements, and affiliate partnerships to distinguish them from editorial content.

Transparent Corrections Policy

When errors occur, we promptly correct them and clearly note the corrections at the bottom of the article.

Outbrain Advertising Policy Compliance

We adhere to all advertising platform guidelines and maintain ethical advertising practices across our platform.
